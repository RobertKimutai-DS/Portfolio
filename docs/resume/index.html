Professional Summary

Software engineer with a technical background in environmental, water, and renewable energy systems. I design and build complete platforms end-to-end: from a national health insurance system architected for 54M citizens (28 API endpoints) to county government revenue tools and IoT sensor networks. My approach combines systems thinking with automation at every layer, whether that means architecting multi-package R backends with CI/CD pipelines, automating hardware design validation through 9-stage simulation workflows, or building self-healing data ingestion systems. I work primarily in R, Python, and TypeScript, and I use AI-augmented development as standard daily practice.
